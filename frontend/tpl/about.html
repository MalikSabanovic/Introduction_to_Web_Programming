New Configurator

The new Lamborghini configurator offers an enhanced, user-friendly experience for customizing your dream car. It allows users to explore a wide range of colors, materials, and finishes in real-time, offering complete control over every detail of the vehicle, from the exterior to the interior. With high-quality 3D visuals and interactive features, the configurator lets you visualize your choices instantly, ensuring a personalized and immersive design process that brings your vision to life.

Ceremony

A Lamborghini ceremony typically refers to special events or unveilings hosted by the brand to celebrate new car launches, milestone achievements, or exclusive collaborations. These ceremonies are characterized by luxury, elegance, and a high level of craftsmanship, often showcasing the latest models in a grand and dramatic fashion. Guests, including celebrities, automotive enthusiasts, and media, are treated to a sophisticated atmosphere with carefully curated presentations, live performances, and exclusive previews of Lamborghini's cutting-edge designs. These events highlight the brand’s commitment to innovation, performance, and luxury.

Custom Interiors

Lamborghini's custom interiors offer luxury and personalization, allowing owners to choose from premium materials like fine leather, Alcantara, and carbon fiber. With options for bespoke stitching, unique color schemes, and advanced tech integration, each interior is crafted to reflect the owner's style and enhance the driving experience.

Lamborghini X Balenciaga

The Lamborghini x Balenciaga collaboration fuses high-performance automotive design with cutting-edge luxury fashion. This exclusive partnership blends Lamborghini's sleek, aerodynamic aesthetics with Balenciaga’s bold, avant-garde style, resulting in a limited-edition collection of apparel and accessories inspired by speed, power, and innovation. Expect futuristic silhouettes, premium materials, and statement pieces that redefine the intersection of luxury cars and high fashion.
